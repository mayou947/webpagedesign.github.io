The Idea

Okay, so you have an idea for a website or an assignment due for Business Web Technologies. You want to make it look professional and easy to use.

What is the first thing you need to work on?

Content - HTML

The aim of design is to enhance the presentation of the content! Content is the most important element of a website.

You hould spend time gathering your content! Thinking about your layout and sketching your ideas on paper.

Presentation - CSS

Styling your content will make your website more useable. For websites we use Cascading Style Sheets (CSS)

CSS is a set of rules that allow you to style content. The rules are element:style pairs, which identify the element and how it itsto be styled
